George Russell

Team Mercedes
Home Country United Kingdom
Date of Birth 2/15/1998
Place of Birth King's Lynn, England

About

George Russell has started his rookie Formula 1 career by out-qualifying his seasoned team mate Robert Kubica at all of the 21 Grand Prix. He put his team Williams back on the podium in 2021 and won his first race with Mercedes in the 2022 season.

George Russell was born in Norfolk, England to Steve and Alison and is the youngest of two siblings. He spent a lot of his time growing up around karting tracks because his older brother Benjy was involved in competitive karting. He attributes the reason for racing under the number 63 in F1 to his brother.

Early Career

Karting

Russell started karting in 2006 and moved up the cadet class by 2009. He became a MSA British Champion and British Open champion. He won championships in both 2010 and 2011. He defended his title in 2012 and graduated from karting in 2013.

Formula Renault and GP3

George Russell made his single seater debut in 2014 for Formula Renault Alps championship. He finished fourth in the championship that year with one podium finish.

In 2017, Russell signed with ART Grand Prix for the GP3 series. He showed great results throughout the championship and win with a large lead in the standings. He had claimed four victories, and eight pole positions.
